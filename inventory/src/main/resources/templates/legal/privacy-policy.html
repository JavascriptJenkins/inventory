1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Techvvs LLC ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our inventory management and e-commerce platform (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

Account Information

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number (for authentication)
  • Business information
  • Profile preferences

Authentication Data

  • OAuth provider information (Google, etc.)
  • Login credentials and session data
  • Two-factor authentication tokens

Business Data

  • Inventory items and UPC codes
  • Product catalogs and menus
  • Transaction records
  • Customer information

Technical Data

  • IP addresses and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage patterns and analytics
  • Error logs and performance data

2.2 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication: Secure JWT tokens stored in HttpOnly cookies
  • Session Management: Maintaining user login state
  • Security: CSRF protection and fraud prevention
  • Analytics: Understanding how users interact with our service
Cookie Security: Our authentication cookies are configured with HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite attributes to maximize security and prevent unauthorized access.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Service Provision: To provide inventory management and e-commerce functionality
  • Authentication: To verify user identity and maintain secure sessions
  • Account Management: To create and manage user accounts
  • Business Operations: To process transactions and manage inventory
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and provide assistance

3.2 Legal and Compliance

  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Tax Reporting: To assist with tax code compliance and reporting
  • Audit Requirements: To maintain records as required by law
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored in cloud-managed databases using industry-standard security practices. We use reputable cloud providers that comply with international security standards.

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure Cookies: HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite cookie attributes
  • HTTPS: All communications encrypted using TLS/SSL

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
  • Meet audit and compliance obligations

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Data Sales: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.1 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our platform (payment processors, cloud hosting, etc.)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: When you have given explicit consent for specific sharing

5.2 Payment Processing

When you integrate payment APIs, those third-party services may collect and process payment information according to their own privacy policies. We do not store credit card information on our servers.
